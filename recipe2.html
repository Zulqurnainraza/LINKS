CHICKEN KHARI

It is the favourite dish pf PAKISTAN and a good dish of ckicken

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken 1kg 16 pieces
  • Black pepper coarsely crushed 1 ½ tsp
  • Ginger garlic 1 tbsp
  • Lemon juice 1 tbsp
  • Salt 1 ½ tsp
  • Yoghurt ½ cups

METHORD

      Marinate chicken with yoghurt, salt and black pepper, lemon juice, ginger garlic. Leave it for 3o min.
      Heat oil, add ginger garlic paste with blended tomatoes, salt, crushed red pepper, brown onion, and marinated chicken, cover and cook till chicken tender.
      Lastly add allspice, coriander leaf and green chilies
